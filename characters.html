Main characters

  • Tanjiro Kamado
    • Role: The main protagonist of the anime and manga Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba
    • Age: At the start of the story – 13 years old, later 15-16 years old
    • Goal: To become a Demon Slayer to save his sister Nezuko and avenge his family, who were killed by a demon
    • Personality: Kind, determined, compassionate, and with a strong sense of justice
    • Abilities:
      • Mastery of Water Breathing and Sun Breathing (Hinokami Kagura)
      • Possesses an acute sense of smell, aiding in detecting threats and tracking
    • Unique Traits: Tanjiro wears a black-and-green checkered haori and wields a katana with a black blade
    • Quote: "When you do good, it comes back to you."
  • Nezuko Kamado
    • Role: Tanjiro's younger sister and a central character in Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba
    • Age: 12 at the start of the story, later 14 (as a demon, she does not age)
    • Goal: To regain her humanity after being turned into a demon
    • Personality: Gentle, caring, and fiercely protective of her brother despite her demon nature
    • Abilities:
      • Enhanced strength, speed, and regenerative abilities as a demon
      • Uses Blood Demon Art: Exploding Blood to attack enemies
    • Unique Traits: Nezuko retains her human emotions and refuses to harm humans, often fighting demons alongside Tanjiro
    • Quote: "I will protect my brother, no matter what."
  • Zenitsu Agatsuma
    • Role: One of Tanjiro’s companions and a member of the Demon Slayer Corps
    • Age: 16 years old
    • Goal: To overcome his cowardice and prove his worth as a demon slayer
    • Personality: Cowardly, anxious, but fiercely loyal and brave when unconscious
    • Abilities:
      • Master of Thunder Breathing, specifically the First Form: Thunderclap and Flash
      • Exceptional speed and precision during battle
    • Unique Traits: Falls asleep during intense fear, allowing his true combat abilities to surface
    • Quote: "Even if I’m scared, I’ll still fight to protect those I care about."
  • Inosuke Hashibira
    • Role: A hot-headed warrior and one of Tanjiro's companions in the Demon Slayer Corps
    • Age: 15 years old
    • Goal: To become the strongest warrior and prove his dominance
    • Personality: Aggressive, competitive, yet loyal to his friends
    • Abilities:
      • Self-created Beast Breathing techniques
      • Extreme flexibility and heightened senses, particularly touch
    • Unique Traits: Wears a boar’s head mask and was raised in the mountains by wild animals
    • Quote: "I’m the strongest, and I’ll show everyone!"
  • Kanao Tsuyuri
    • Role: A skilled Demon Slayer and Shinobu Kocho’s protégé
    • Age: 16 years old
    • Goal: To overcome her indecisiveness and follow her heart
    • Personality: Quiet, reserved, and often relies on a coin flip to make decisions
    • Abilities:
      • Mastery of Flower Breathing techniques
      • Exceptional agility and sharp eyesight in combat
    • Unique Traits: Carries a coin given to her by Shinobu and Kanae, which symbolizes her struggle to make choices
    • Quote: "I’ll decide what to do when the time comes."
  • Genya Shinazugawa
    • Role: A Demon Slayer and Sanemi Shinazugawa’s younger brother
    • Age: 16 years old
    • Goal: To grow stronger and earn his brother’s acknowledgment
    • Personality: Gruff, temperamental, but deeply caring underneath
    • Abilities:
      • Unique ability to temporarily gain demon powers by consuming demon flesh
      • Proficient with firearms and close combat
    • Unique Traits: Does not use traditional breathing styles, relying instead on his demonic abilities and brute strength
    • Quote: "I’ll protect others, no matter the cost to myself."

Hashira characters

  • Giyu Tomioka
    • Role: Water Hashira (Pillar) in the Demon Slayer Corps
    • Age: Around 21 years old
    • Goal: To protect humanity from demons and uphold the Demon Slayer Corps’ mission
    • Personality: Reserved, stoic, and highly disciplined, but deeply cares for others
    • Abilities:
      • Mastery of Water Breathing techniques, including the unique Eleventh Form: Dead Calm
      • Exceptional swordsmanship and tactical thinking in combat
    • Unique Traits: Wears a distinct haori, half green and half patterned in honor of his late friend Sabito
    • Quote: "Those who cannot protect themselves have no right to criticize those who protect them."
  • Shinobu Kocho
    • Role: Insect Hashira (Pillar) in the Demon Slayer Corps
    • Age: Around 18 years old
    • Goal: To avenge her sister Kanae's death and create a world free of demons
    • Personality: Cheerful and polite but hides her deep resentment towards demons
    • Abilities:
      • Mastery of Insect Breathing, utilizing swift, precise thrusts with a blade laced with poison
      • Skilled in creating and using wisteria-based poison against demons
    • Unique Traits: Her blade is specially designed to inject poison rather than cut
    • Quote: "I can't bring myself to hate you, but that doesn't mean I will forgive you."
  • Kyojuro Rengoku
    • Role: Flame Hashira (Pillar) in the Demon Slayer Corps
    • Age: Around 20 years old
    • Goal: To protect the weak and live up to his father’s legacy as a Hashira
    • Personality: Passionate, optimistic, and courageous, with a strong sense of duty
    • Abilities:
      • Mastery of Flame Breathing techniques, including Ninth Form: Rengoku
      • Incredible endurance and unshakable willpower
    • Unique Traits: Known for his fiery spirit and iconic flame-patterned haori
    • Quote: "Set your heart ablaze."
  • Muichiro Tokito
    • Role: Mist Hashira (Pillar) in the Demon Slayer Corps
    • Age: 14 years old
    • Goal: To protect humanity and uncover his forgotten past
    • Personality: Calm, detached, and often lost in thought, but highly focused in battle
    • Abilities:
      • Mastery of Mist Breathing techniques, emphasizing agility and disorientation
      • Exceptional reflexes and a keen analytical mind
    • Unique Traits: Despite his young age, he is one of the most skilled Hashira
    • Quote: "The mist may obscure your vision, but clarity will always come with focus."
  • Mitsuri Kanroji
    • Role: Love Hashira (Pillar) in the Demon Slayer Corps
    • Age: Around 19 years old
    • Goal: To fight for those she loves and find her place in the world
    • Personality: Kind, cheerful, and emotional, with a strong sense of loyalty
    • Abilities:
      • Mastery of Love Breathing techniques, combining agility and flexibility
      • Extraordinary physical strength, despite her delicate appearance
    • Unique Traits: Wields a unique, ultra-thin whip-like katana
    • Quote: "Love is what gives us the strength to keep going."
  • Gyomei Himejima
    • Role: Stone Hashira (Pillar) in the Demon Slayer Corps
    • Age: 27 years old
    • Goal: To protect humanity and honor his duty as a Hashira
    • Personality: Gentle, deeply spiritual, and empathetic, yet a fearsome warrior
    • Abilities:
      • Mastery of Stone Breathing, utilizing powerful and destructive techniques
      • Impressive physical strength and combat skills, heightened by his blindness
    • Unique Traits: Wields a spiked flail and axe connected by a chain, a unique weapon among the Hashira
    • Quote: "Strength is born from faith and perseverance."
  • Sanemi Shinazugawa
    • Role: Wind Hashira (Pillar) in the Demon Slayer Corps
    • Age: 21 years old
    • Goal: To eradicate demons and protect his comrades
    • Personality: Fierce, abrasive, and hot-tempered, but deeply loyal and protective
    • Abilities:
      • Mastery of Wind Breathing, using aggressive and dynamic techniques
      • Enhanced agility and exceptional combat instincts
    • Unique Traits: Known for his scars and wild demeanor, reflecting his intense battles
    • Quote: "I'll risk everything to protect what matters most."
  • Obanai Iguro
    • Role: Serpent Hashira (Pillar) in the Demon Slayer Corps
    • Age: 21 years old
    • Goal: To defeat Muzan Kibutsuji and ensure the survival of humanity
    • Personality: Strict, serious, and reserved, with a strong sense of duty
    • Abilities:
      • Mastery of Serpent Breathing, using slithering, unpredictable sword movements
      • Accompanied by a pet snake, Kaburamaru, which aids him in battle
    • Unique Traits: Wears bandages over his face to hide scars; his heterochromatic eyes are distinct
    • Quote: "Justice requires sacrifice."
  • Tengen Uzui
    • Role: Sound Hashira (Pillar) in the Demon Slayer Corps
    • Age: 23 years old
    • Goal: To defeat demons with flair and protect his three wives and comrades
    • Personality: Flashy, confident, and flamboyant, but deeply caring for those he protects
    • Abilities:
      • Mastery of Sound Breathing, utilizing rhythmic attacks and explosions
      • Exceptional physical strength and agility, as well as strategic thinking in combat
    • Unique Traits: Wields dual Nichirin cleavers connected by a chain; his flamboyant personality is iconic
    • Quote: "A flashy victory is the only kind worth having!"
