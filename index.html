About anime

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a popular Japanese anime based on the manga by Koyoharu Gotouge. It follows the story of Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a Demon Slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons, and his sister Nezuko is turned into one. Determined to find a cure for Nezuko and avenge his family, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps, where he trains to fight powerful demons using a technique called Breathing Styles. The series is set in Taisho-era Japan and features intense battles, stunning animation, and emotional storytelling. It explores themes of family, perseverance, and the battle between good and evil. The anime has gained immense popularity worldwide for its breathtaking visuals, created by Ufotable, and its deeply engaging characters. More..
